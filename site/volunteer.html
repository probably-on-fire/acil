Volunteer with ACIL

Every paver, every event, every cleared trail — ACIL runs on volunteer time. If you've got a few hours, we have plenty of ways to put them to use.

How to get involved

The fastest way to volunteer is to email anzacivic@gmail.com with your name, contact info, and what you'd like to help with. A board member will follow up.

You can also show up to any monthly board meeting (see the calendar) and introduce yourself — public welcome.

What we need help with

  • Park work days — quarterly clean-up, brush clearing, painting, light maintenance.
  • Anza Days — set-up the day before, parade marshalling, vendor check-in, food booth, tear-down.
  • Schoolhouse upkeep — interior cleaning, light fixture maintenance, decorative care.
  • Event support — opening/closing the schoolhouse for rentals, walk-through with renters.
  • Board service — there is currently a vacant Director seat on the 2026 board. Interested? Reach out.

Liability & safety

For any volunteer activity involving tools, climbing, or driving, ACIL asks volunteers to sign a basic liability waiver before participating. We'll provide the form on-site. Anza Days and routine work days typically don't require waivers in advance.
