Rent Minor Park or the Schoolhouse

Host your event in a 110-year-old landmark surrounded by mountain views. Check availability, fill out an application, and we'll be in touch.

Please see our calendar to verify that the date of your event or meeting is available. Then email anzacivic@gmail.com or phone (951) 330-4411 to schedule your event.

Rentals are not confirmed or finalized until all paperwork and fees have been received.

Terms & rules of use

Renters must agree to abide by the rules outlined in the rental application, including:

  • No open flames inside the Schoolhouse.
  • Alcohol is permitted with proper notification; ACIL may require additional insurance.
  • The renter is responsible for cleanup, trash removal, and any damages.
  • Music and amplified sound must comply with local noise ordinances.
  • All deposits must be received before the event date.

For full terms, see the rental application document. Have questions? Email anzacivic@gmail.com.
