Little Red Schoolhouse or Minor Park - Terms & Rules of Use

  • Renter shall dispose of all trash accumulated during their usage.
  • Renter shall leave the restrooms in a clean state. Gate valves are located on the exterior of each restroom. It is the responsibility of the Renter to open the valves when the event starts and to close the valves after the event.
  • Renter may move furnishings (tables/chairs/desks) to accommodate their event; however, all furnishings are to be reasonably returned to their original location. Brooms are located within the schoolhouse for your convenience.  Please sweep clean after your event and turn off all circuit breakers except the one marked “wi-fi”.
  • A refundable security deposit shall be required with all reservations. Any and all costs of clean-up, damage, lost or unreturned keys, broken lights, etc incurred by ACIL as a result of the event shall be withheld from the deposit. Extraordinary expenses due to damages or negligence will be invoiced separately and will be due and payable upon receipt.
  • The Wi-Fi Hotspot is provided free of charge. No password is required.
  • Renters may be required to provide proof of liability insurance with a minimum of $1,000,000 coverage with “Anza Civic Improvement League (ACIL), 56481 Highway 371, Anza CA 92539” indicated as the “additional insured”. Please contact us at anzacivic@gmail.com if you would like to receive a referral to insurance companies that may be able to provide this type of coverage.

  • All park renters with vendors shall reimburse ACIL $10 per vendor requesting use of electricity. Renter shall notify ACIL of electricity requirements at least 14 days in advance of the event so we can accommodate for 120V or 220V, and assist with placement of such vendors within the park.
