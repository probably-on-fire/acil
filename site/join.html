Become a member of ACIL

Your membership keeps Minor Park and the Little Red Schoolhouse open, maintained, and available to everyone.

Your participation and financial support of our community park helps make it available for all to enjoy. Every member strengthens the League's commitment to maintain the historic Little Red Schoolhouse as well as Minor Park.

Even when you don't have the time to be personally involved, your membership dues and donations support the volunteers who maintain and improve the facilities. As a community member, you make everything we do possible.
