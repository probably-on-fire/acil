Purpose

Anza Civic Improvement League (ACIL) owns and maintains Minor Park in Anza, CA.
The Little Red Schoolhouse, built in 1914, is located within the park.
Both the park and the schoolhouse are available to rent for meetings and events.
ACIL is a 501(c)3 non-profit corporation.

Mission Statement

The mission of the Anza Civic Improvement League is to restore, manage and enhance
the Little Red Schoolhouse and Minor Park in partnership with the public,
for the enjoyment of present and future generations.
