Our Purpose

Restored. Stewarded. Open to all.

ACIL owns and maintains Minor Park in Anza, California. The Little Red Schoolhouse, built in 1914, sits within the park. Both are available to rent for weddings, meetings, and gatherings. We are a 501(c)(3) nonprofit, run by neighbors.
