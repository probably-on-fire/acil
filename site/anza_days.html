Anza Days

One day a year, the whole valley comes to Minor Park — for the parade, the vendors, the food, and the friends you haven't seen in months.

Anza Days is the highlight of our calendar — an all-day celebration at Minor Park featuring a morning parade, community vendors, food, music, and family activities. Admission is free; everyone is welcome.
